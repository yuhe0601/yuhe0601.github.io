Projects

These are my research projects and ongoing work.

2025

National Natural Science Foundation of China (NSFC)
⭐ Large-Scale Models for Ceramic Artifact Virtual Restoration with Suppressed Cultural Bias and Hallucination
Period: 2026.01.01–2029.12.31
Funding: RMB 500,000
Role: Principal Investigator
We focus on enabling intelligent virtual restoration of cultural relics by addressing cultural bias, cross-modal hallucinations, and excessive imagination in generative models, through a domain knowledge-driven 3D content generation framework that integrates dynamic feature regulation, cultural-context-aware representation learning, multi-task knowledge distillation, and expert-in-the-loop evaluation to ensure fact-consistent and reliable restoration under low-resource conditions.
Natural Science Foundation of Shaanxi Province (Key Project)
**Key Techniques for Virtual Restoration of Severely Damaged Cultural Artifacts Based on Zero-Shot Learning**
Period: 2025.01.01–2027.12.31
Funding: RMB 200,000
Role: Principal Investigator
We focus on virtual restoration of severely damaged cultural artifacts by replacing end-to-end feature matching with a feature-complementary framework for multimodal feature learning, completion, and prediction, enabling zero-shot restoration under missing data and sparse training conditions.

2019

National Natural Science Foundation of China (NSFC)
⭐ Automatic Reassembly of Fragmented Artifacts Based on Deep Feature Pattern Modeling
Period: 2019.01.01–2021.12.31
Funding: RMB 240,000
Role: Principal Investigator
We focus on the automatic reassembly of defective cultural relic fragments by addressing three key challenges: deep feature pattern learning with graph-based representation, probabilistic fragment matching, and reassembly optimization. This work establishes a unified framework that advances beyond single-feature matching and supports digital preservation, virtual restoration, and related areas such as deep learning and geometric processing.
